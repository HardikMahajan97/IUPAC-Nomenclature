IUPAC Nomenclature

Some Common Functional Groups

  1. Alcohols (-OH):

    Structural Formula: R-OH

    Examples: Ethanol (C2H5OH),
    Methanol (CH3OH),
    Propanol (C3H7OH),
    Butanol (C4H9OH),
    Pentanol (C5H11OH)

  2. Aldehydes (-CHO):

    Structural Formula: R-CHO

    Examples: Formaldehyde (HCHO),
    Acetaldehyde (CH3CHO),
    Propionaldehyde (C3H7CHO),
    Butyraldehyde (C4H9CHO),
    Valeraldehyde (C5H11CHO)

  3. Ketones (-C=O):

    Structural Formula: R-C=O-R'

    Examples: Acetone (CH3COCH3),
    Propanone (CH3COCH2CH3),
    Butanone (CH3COCH2CH2CH3),
    Pentan-3-one (CH3COCH2CH2CH2CH3),
    Hexan-3-one (CH3COCH2CH2CH2CH2CH3)

  4. Carboxylic Acids (-COOH):

    Structural Formula: R-COOH

    Examples: Acetic acid (CH3COOH),
    Formic acid (HCOOH),
    Propionic acid (C3H7COOH),
    Butyric acid (C4H9COOH),
    Benzoic acid (C6H5COOH)

  5. Esters (-COO-):

    Structural Formula: R-COO-R'

    Examples: Ethyl acetate (CH3COOCH2CH3),
    Methyl butanoate (CH3COOCH2CH2CH3),
    Propyl ethanoate (CH3COOCH2CH2CH2CH3),
    Butyl propanoate (CH3COOCH2CH2CH2CH2CH3),
    Pentyl methanoate (CH3COOCH2CH2CH2CH2CH2CH3)

  6. Amines (-NH2):

    Structural Formula: R-NH2

    Examples: Methylamine (CH3NH2),
    Ethylamine (C2H5NH2),
    Propylamine (C3H7NH2),
    Butylamine (C4H9NH2),
    Pentylamine (C5H11NH2)

  7. Amides (-CONH2):

    Structural Formula: R-CONH2

    Examples: Acetamide (CH3CONH2),
    Propionamide (CH3CH2CONH2),
    Butyramide (CH3CH2CH2CONH2),
    N-Methylacetamide (CH3CONHCH3),
    N-Ethylpropanamide (CH3CH2CH2CONH2)

  8. Nitriles (-CN):

    Structural Formula: R-CN

    Examples: Acetonitrile (CH3CN),
    Propionitrile (CH3CH2CN),
    Butyronitrile (CH3CH2CH2CN),
    Valeronitrile (CH3CH2CH2CH2CN),
    Benzonitrile (C6H5CN)

  9. Halides (-X, where X = F, Cl, Br, I):

    Structural Formula: R-X

    Examples: Chloromethane (CH3Cl),
    Bromoethane (CH3CH2Br),
    Iodopropane (CH3CH2CH2I),
    Fluorobutane (CH3CH2CH2CH2F),
    Chloropentane (CH3CH2CH2CH2CH2Cl)

  10. Ethers (-O-):

    Structural Formula: R-O-R'

    Examples: Dimethyl ether (CH3OCH3),
    Diethyl ether (C2H5OC2H5),
    Methyl ethyl ether (CH3OC2H5),
    Propyl isopropyl ether (C3H7OC3H7),
    Butyl butyl ether (C4H9OC4H9)

  11. Sulfides (-S-):

    Structural Formula: R-S-R'

    Examples: Dimethyl sulfide (CH3SCH3),
    Diethyl sulfide (C2H5SCH2C2H5),
    Methyl propyl sulfide (CH3SCH2CH2CH3),
    Ethyl butyl sulfide (C2H5SCH2CH2CH2CH3),
    Propyl isopropyl sulfide (CH3SCH2CH(CH3)CH3)

  12. Phenols (-OH attached to a benzene ring):

    Structural Formula: Ar-OH

    Examples: Phenol (C6H5OH),
    O-Cresol (CH3C6H4OH),
    P-Cresol (CH3C6H4OH),
    Guaiacol (C6H3(OH)CH3),
    Eugenol (C6H3(OH)(OCH3)CH2CH2CH3)

  13. Aromatic Amines (NH2 attached to a benzene ring):

    Structural Formula: Ar-NH2

    Examples: Aniline (C6H5NH2),
    Toluidine (CH3C6H4NH2),
    Xylidine (C6H3(CH3)2NH2),
    Diphenylamine (C6H5NHC6H5),
    Naphthylamine (C10H7NH2)

  14. Nitrates (-NO2):

    Structural Formula: Ar-NO2

    Examples: Nitrobenzene (C6H5NO2), Methyl nitrobenzene (CH3C6H4NO2), Ethyl nitrobenzene (C2H5C6H4NO2)
